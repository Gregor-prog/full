How Does It Work

EduGaze brings learning to life through interactive 3D experiences.

01. Scan and Project in AR

Students simply scan a real-world surface using their device's camera, and EduGaze anchors a 3D model onto it. This brings static concepts to life by placing virtual objects in real-world environments.

03. Explore in 360°

Learners can walk around or move their devices to examine 3D models from any angle. This full spatial freedom deepens their understanding of shapes, anatomy, machinery, or environments like ancient ruins or planetary systems.

02. Interact and Experiment

Students can rotate, zoom, and even dissect 3D objects. In virtual labs, they can simulate scientific experiments—like mixing chemicals or building circuits—without the need for physical materials.

04. Understand Through Immersion

By immersing students in virtual environments—microscopic worlds, outer space, or historical landmarks—EduGaze enables them to grasp abstract or complex ideas through direct experience and spatial awareness.
